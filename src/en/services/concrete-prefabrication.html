Concrete Prefabrication

Custom bent bars, stirrups and reinforcing meshes.

About the service

Speed and strength for modern construction

Our production capacity supports both serial and project-based manufacture of reinforcement and concrete components, forming the foundation of reliable commercial and private construction.

Standardised processes guarantee dimensional repeatability. Our own transport fleet ensures on-time delivery to construction sites in Poland and Germany.

How we work

Reinforcement process

01

Estimating and detailing

Analysis of reinforcement drawings and steel schedules, including BVBS data preparation for bending machines.

02

Forming and processing

Automated lines bend, cut and assemble ribbed bars into the required reinforcement geometry.

03

Logistics and installation

Delivery by truck and crane-equipped trailers, on time and directly to the specified site in Poland or Germany.

What we produce

Our products

Bent and cut bars

Reinforcement bars formed exactly to the shape specified by the structural engineer.

Stirrups and reinforcement cages

Repeatable reinforcement components for strip foundations and reinforced concrete columns.

Reinforcing meshes

Meshes for slabs and floors that reduce stress concentrations within concrete layers.
