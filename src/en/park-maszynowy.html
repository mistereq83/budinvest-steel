Technical facilities

Our machinery

Where precision meets modern technology.

BUD-INVEST operates a modern production hall equipped with professional metalworking, welding and prefabrication machinery. Our technical facilities allow us to deliver highly complex projects, from precision TIG welding to assembly of heavy Steel Structures.

Space and capabilities

Assembly hall and welding shop

Modern production hall with full equipment. MIG/MAG, TIG and electrode welding stations, overhead cranes, hoists and assembly tables. The facility is adapted for large components such as Steel Structures, vessels and pipelines.
