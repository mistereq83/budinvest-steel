Industrial Pipelines

Piping systems for the chemical, petrochemical and power industries.

About the service

From prefabrication to final assembly

We build complete piping systems, from small installation diameters to large-diameter process pipelines. Prefabrication takes place in our workshop and assembly at the client’s facility.

We weld stainless, acid-resistant and special steels. Every weld is quality-controlled in accordance with EN ISO 3834.

Process

Project stages

01

Isometric analysis

Verification of isometric drawings, material selection and valve specification, followed by costing and scheduling.

02

Prefabrication

Cutting, bending, welding and pressure testing in controlled workshop conditions.

03

Assembly and handover

Site installation, leak testing and as-built documentation for Poland and Germany.
