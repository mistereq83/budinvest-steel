Pressure Vessels

About the service

Vessels for demanding media

We manufacture pressure and atmospheric vessels for the chemical, petrochemical, energy and food-processing industries. We work with stainless steel, acid-resistant steel and special alloys.

Every vessel is controlled through NDT weld inspection, pressure testing and acceptance procedures compliant with European standards.

Process

Production stages

01

Documentation and costing

Review of technical specifications, material selection and welding technology definition. Quotation within 48 hours.

02

Fabrication and inspection

Rolling, welding and heat treatment, followed by NDT testing: RT, UT, PT and MT, plus pressure tests.

03

Delivery and assembly

Heavy transport, site assembly, as-built documentation and handover certificates.
