Steel Structures

About the service

Complete delivery from A to Z

We design, prefabricate and assemble Steel Structures for industrial and construction projects. Our own production hall in Tczew gives us full control over the process.

EN 1090 certification allows us to manufacture load-bearing structures in EXC2 and EXC3 classes, from industrial halls to components for process installations.
